Places to Visit in Illinois

1.Downtown Chicago

Chicago, Illinois is my favorite place to visit in Illinois. It has a beautiful skyline and lots to sightsee. There are tons of museums and places to shop.

Some of my favorite places to visit include:

  • The Lake Front
  • The Magnificent Mile
  • The Chicago River

2.Six Flags Great America

Six Flags is a great amusement park located in Gurnee Illinois. It is one of twenty seven locations in the United States with over 50 roller coasters. Not only does it have fun roller coasters, but there are fun carnival games and shows.

Some of my favorite rides include:

  • Raging Bull
  • Goliath
  • SuperMan
  • X-Flight

3.Naperville

Naperville is five miles west of Chicago, and one of the city's most popular suburbs. The Naperville River winds its way through the city, with numerous attractions on either side of the river and in nature. Head to the famous Centennial Beach or check out downtown for boutique shopping and eateries.

Some places to see in Naperville are:

  • Naperville River Walk
  • Naperville Public Library
  • Dave's Hot Chicken
